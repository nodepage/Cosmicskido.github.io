About us

What Is Cosmic kid?

Cosmic Kid Coin ($CSC) draws inspiration from the iconic animated series "Kid Cosmic," created by Craig McCracken for Netflix. This captivating show chronicles the thrilling adventures of Kid and his friends as they navigate cosmic escapades fueled by courage and imagination.

Just like the spirited gang from the series, Cosmic Kid Coin ($CSC) is committed to preserving the essence of friendship, bravery, and boundless exploration found in Kid Cosmic's universe. Our token serves as a digital tribute, commemorating the cherished memories of fans.

With features inspired by the show's themes of teamwork and discovery, coupled with community governance, Cosmic Kid Coin ($CSC) becomes a powerful vessel for engagement and celebration among the Kid Cosmic fanbase. Join us as we embark on cosmic escapades together!
